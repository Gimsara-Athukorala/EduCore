## The Perfect 2-Minute Demo

### PART 1: Introduction (15 seconds)
**What to Say:**
"I've built a Study Material Repository for our LMS. Students can browse and upload three types of materials: Past Papers, Short Notes, and Kuppi Videos, organized by year, semester, and module."

**What to Show:**
- Point to the header showing all three material types
- Show the year selection cards

---

### PART 2: Demonstrate Hierarchy (30 seconds)
**What to Say:**
"The system follows the exact university curriculum structure."

**What to Do:**
1. Click **"Year 3"** (shows you're in the advanced level)
2. Click **"Semester 1"**
3. Show the module list: "Here are all modules for Year 3, Semester 1"
4. Click **"IT3020 - Machine Learning"**

**Key Point to Mention:**
"Notice the clean navigation and professional interface throughout."

---

### PART 3: View Materials with Tabs (25 seconds)
**What to Say:**
"Each module has three distinct sections for different material types."

**What to Do:**
1. Click **"Past Papers"** tab - show existing papers
2. Click **"Short Notes"** tab - show notes
3. Click **"Kuppi Videos"** tab - show videos
4. Click one material to show it opens

**Key Point to Mention:**
"All interfaces are complete and functional. Students can click any material to access it."

---

### PART 4: Upload Form Demo (40 seconds)
**What to Say:**
"Now I'll demonstrate the upload functionality with comprehensive validations."

**What to Do:**
1. Click **"Upload Material"** button
2. **IMMEDIATELY** click **"Populate Dummy Data"** button
   - Point out: "This feature saves time by filling the form with valid data"
3. Quickly show the populated fields:
   - Title: ✓ Proper capitalization
   - Type: ✓ Selected
   - URL: ✓ Valid format
   - Description: ✓ Complete
4. Click **"Upload Material"**
5. Wait for success message

**What to Say:**
"Notice all validations are working - required fields, URL format checking, minimum length requirements."

---

### PART 5: Verify and Conclude (10 seconds)
**What to Do:**
1. After upload success, the material appears in the list
2. Scroll to show the new material

**What to Say:**
"The material is now available to all students. This demonstrates over 35% working functionality with complete interfaces and proper validations."

---

## Grading Criteria Coverage

When presenting, you're demonstrating:

1. **Complete Interfaces** ✓
   - Year selector
   - Semester selector
   - Module list
   - Material viewer with tabs
   - Upload form

2. **Form Validations** ✓
   - Show the populated form has all valid data
   - Mention: "Required fields, URL validation, length checks"

3. **35% Functionality** ✓
   - Viewing materials (works)
   - Uploading materials (works)
   - Navigation (works)
   - Database integration (works)

4. **Proper Capitalization** ✓
   - All labels use correct case
   - "Past Papers" not "past papers"
   - "Short Notes" not "short notes"

5. **Dummy Data Button** ✓
   - One-click form population
   - Saves presentation time
